# Sector 04 — Cycles and Waves

**Mission:** Prove the transmitter's circle and the receiver's wave are the same signal.

**Key idea:**

- Walk the unit circle counterclockwise from `(1, 0)` by angle `θ` (radians). Your position is `(cos θ, sin θ)` — that's the definition.
- Freeze just the height and unroll it over time — that traces the sine wave. The wave is the circle's shadow.

**Your task:** Drag the angle around the circle. Match the target angle when asked.
